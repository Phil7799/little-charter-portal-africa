Data Structure

The portal expects an Excel file with three sheets:

  • Sheet 1 (charter): Monthly revenue by associate with new/existing/busbuddy breakdown, 2025 & 2026 data with targets
  • Sheet 2 (hr): HR services revenue — Month, Business, 2026 Target, 2026 Actual
  • Sheet 3 (tele): Weekly telesales data — WeekNumber, BusBuddy & Taxi meetings booked/attended/closed/trials
