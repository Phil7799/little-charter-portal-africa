Data Structure

The portal expects an Excel file with two sheets:

  • Sheet 1 (charter): Monthly revenue data by associate for 2025 and 2026 with targets
  • Sheet 2 (hr): HR services revenue data with Month, Business, 2026 Target, and 2026 Actual columns

Download a sample data template to see the required format.
