File Format Instructions

Your Excel file must contain two sheets with the following structure:

Sheet 1: Named "charter" (or "Charter" or "Sheet1")

Required columns: Month, Associate, Total Revenue_2025, Total Revenue_2026, Target 2026

Note: Column names are flexible - the system will match variations.

Sheet 2: Named "hr" (or "HR" or "Sheet2") - NEW STRUCTURE

Required columns: Month, Business, 2026 Target, 2026 Actual

Business values: Overall, Payroll System, School Attendance System, TELESALES - Payroll System

Important: Each month should have rows for each business type.

Example HR Data:

Month       Business                      2026 Target    2026 Actual

January     Payroll System                200,000.00     0.00
January     School Attendance System      0.00           0.00
